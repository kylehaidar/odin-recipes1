How do we make this? (extremely hard)

  1. Boil some water!
  2. Add your beautiful noodles
  3. Now strain your noodles
  4. Lower the heat on your pot
  5. Add half a stick of butter and some milk to it, dont add your noodles back just yet!
  6. wait for the butter to melt, and add the cheese packet
  7. mixxxxx
  8. Now, add your noodles back and stir it all together
  9. Now comes the finishing touches, add some of the mexican cheese to it and stir!
  10. Here is the cherry on top. Put the heat to high, and add some more cheese to the very top and don't stir!
  11. Let that go on for like 20 seconds, and voila, youre done!
