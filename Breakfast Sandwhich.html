A College Essential!

Oooh, what do we got here you may ask? Now, this is a legendary meal in the Liver King household, this meal is specially crafted to my picky taste buds, and it is a college essential, it is extremely cheap, tasty, and quick! This is a breakfast sandwich on a plain bagel including an egg, some cheese, and some turkey! Man im getting hungry even thinking about this. This is such an easy meal to make, and a meal I have made multiple times in one day, it is delicious. Picture is not true size...or true dish.

Now what do we need to make this beautiful thing?

  • A plain bagel
  • Butter
  • An egg
  • Deli slices of turkey
  • Any seasonings you like!
  • Franks red hot

Okay, I know, you might be thinking, "wow, is that all? This wont be good at all" Haha, wait till you see... im also an extremely picky eater

Anyway, how do we make one? Ill show you!

  1. Well, pretty self explanatory, toast your damn bagel! (dont burn it, made that mistake too many times)
  2. While your bagel is toasting, eat up a pan with a little bit of butter, and put that heat up to medium high heat!
  3. Crack your egg in the middle of the pan, and get ready
  4. Season your egg with anything you would like
  5. Around here, we fucking hate yolk, so you are gonna cook that so its well done!
  6. Now of course, dont forget, we dont have spatulas in the Liver King household, so flip that bad boy, and dont mess up.
  7. Now, while you are cooking your egg, butter your damn bagel!
  8. While that is almost done, take four pieces of cheap ass sliced turkey, and put that in the microwave for 15 seconds.
  9. You are almost done here!
  10. Place your buttered bagel onto a plate, slide the egg on, and place your turkey!
  11. See, you might be done here, but I am not, I put some franks red hot on that thang, and it is a game changer
  12. Now you are done!

Give this a try, and if you say you dont like it, you are getting a hit put on you for 6 dollars, because that is all you are worth. Enjoy!
